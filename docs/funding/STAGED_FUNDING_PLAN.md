# Staged Funding Plan

## Goal

Raise support in practical stages rather than asking for a full experimental campaign up front.

## Why This Structure Works

The first hard gate is whether a qualified lab can synthesize the material or a close equivalent under realistic conditions. Without that, later experimental spending is premature.

## Recommended Funding Stages

### Stage 1

Obtain a formal synthesis quote and fund initial material production.

### Stage 2

Fund PXRD confirmation so the produced material can be checked against the expected framework before deeper testing.

### Stage 3

Fund activation and sample-quality verification to confirm the material is actually ready for gas testing.

### Stage 4

Fund the first hydrogen adsorption measurement under the most decision-useful conditions.

## Core Rule

- no physical sample means no experimental PXRD
- no PXRD match means be cautious about paying for hydrogen testing
- a good PXRD match justifies moving to activation and adsorption work

## Best Framing

This project should be presented as a disciplined, milestone-based validation effort rather than a request to fund a speculative open-ended program.
