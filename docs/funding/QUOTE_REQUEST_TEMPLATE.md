# Quote Request Template

Use this template as a public example for an initial quote discussion. Keep any named contacts, private notes, and routing decisions outside the repository.

## Subject Line Options

- Confidential inquiry: custom synthesis fit for porous framework candidate
- Request for confidential quote discussion: hydrogen-storage MOF candidate
- Initial fit check under confidentiality for specialty framework synthesis

## Email Draft

Hello,

I am an independent researcher preparing a staged validation effort for a hydrogen-storage porous framework candidate. I am looking for a synthesis partner who can first confirm technical fit for a confidential quote discussion.

At this stage, I am intentionally not circulating the full structural package. Before sharing detailed files, I would like to know whether your team is open to handling a confidential custom synthesis request for a framework material and whether you support confidentiality terms or an NDA before detailed disclosure.

The immediate goal is Stage 1 of a disciplined validation plan:

- obtain a real synthesis quote
- determine whether the target material is practically synthesizable
- produce a physical sample for downstream structural confirmation

If there is a fit and confidentiality is in place, I can then provide the technical files needed for evaluation.

Best regards,

[Your name]

## Ask Them Specifically

- whether they handle confidential custom synthesis requests for porous or MOF-like materials
- whether they accept low-volume exploratory projects
- what minimum batch size or project scope they require
- whether they can support only synthesis or also PXRD and activation
- whether they can provide a formal quote or at least an initial estimate
