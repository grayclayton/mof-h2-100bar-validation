# Stage 1 Crowdfunding Draft

## Working Title

Stage 1: Synthesize a promising hydrogen-storage MOF candidate for real-world testing

## Short Summary

This project funds the first real-world gate for a hydrogen-storage material candidate: making the physical material. The candidate has already been narrowed computationally, but no experimental validation can begin until a lab actually synthesizes the target framework.

## What Stage 1 Funds

- obtaining one or more real synthesis quotes
- selecting a synthesis partner
- producing the physical material sample
- documenting whether synthesis succeeded, failed, or required target adjustments

## Why This Stage Matters

Without a physical sample, there is no way to run experimental PXRD, activation, or hydrogen uptake measurements. This stage is intentionally narrow and is designed to reduce uncertainty before deeper spending.

## Deliverables

- at least one real synthesis quote or work scope
- a documented decision on the selected synthesis route
- a produced sample if synthesis succeeds
- a short report explaining the outcome and next recommended step
