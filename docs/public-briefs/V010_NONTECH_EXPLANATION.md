# How V010 Was Changed From The Base MOF

V010 was created from the base MOF through two controlled modifications.

First, the framework was expanded uniformly. In practice, that means the unit cell was scaled equally in all three directions so the internal pore space became larger. In the locked version used for this project, the scale factor is 1.2600.

Second, 12 of the 48 copper sites were replaced with nickel. That corresponds to a 25 percent Cu-to-Ni substitution at the metal nodes.

The overall topology was not redesigned. The framework angles were preserved and the organic portion of the structure was not fundamentally changed.

In plain language, V010 is the base framework with a larger open structure and a partial Cu/Ni metal substitution.
