# Code Of Conduct

This project is intended to support serious, respectful scientific and technical collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

Participants should:

- discuss ideas in good faith
- challenge claims with evidence and reasoning
- respect uncertainty and incomplete results
- keep criticism technical rather than personal
- avoid overstating conclusions

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not acceptable:

- harassment or intimidation
- personal attacks
- knowingly misleading claims about results
- abusive, discriminatory, or hostile behavior
- unsafe recommendations presented casually or recklessly

## Enforcement

Contributors who repeatedly violate these expectations may be blocked from participation.
